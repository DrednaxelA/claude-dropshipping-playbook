Phase 01 · Research & Validation

Finding Your Niche,
Product & Market

This phase determines everything. Get it wrong and no amount of good marketing saves you. Claude can dramatically accelerate every part of it.

⚠ The most common mistake

People skip research because it feels slow. They pick a product they "like" and build a store before validating demand, margins, or competition. Don't. Research is the only phase where Claude saves you from losing real money.

Before you start anything

Set up a Claude Project together. Call it "Dropshipping HQ". Add a system prompt with: your niche direction, your target customer sketch, your budget, your split of responsibilities, and any constraints (e.g. "We want UK-based suppliers where possible"). Every conversation you have inside this project inherits that context.

Your unfair advantage

You and your partner split the work — one owns marketing/copy, one owns ops/systems. Your background in conversation design and AI management means you're better placed than most to build the customer service automation layer (chatbot flows, email automation, CS templates) that most dropshippers do badly or ignore. That's where your Fin/Intercom skills translate directly.

⚠ The one thing that will kill this early

Skipping research to get to "the fun part" (building the store, running ads). Most dropshipping businesses fail at product selection, not execution. Use Claude for the research phase longer than feels comfortable. Validate before you build.
